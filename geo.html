Layers of atmosphere

There are 6 layers of atmosphere, Troposphere, stratosphere, Mesosphere, Ionosphere, Thermosphere and exosphere.Stratosphere consists ozone which is called ozonosphere and is also ideal for flying of jet aircrafts as it is free from dust particles.The outer part of exosphere is called Magnetosphere.

What are Deltas?

A river delta is a landform created by deposition of sediments that is carried by a river as the flow reaches its mouth and enters slower-moving or stagnant water. This occurs where a river enters an ocean, sea, estuary, lake, reservoir, or another river that cannot carry away the sediments.Sundarbans on the Bay of Bengal are the biggest delta in the world.

The 2 soltices

“Solstice” means sun-stopping. The point on the horizon where the sun appears to rise and set, stops and reverses direction after this day. On the solstice, the sun does not rise precisely in the east, but rises to the north of east and sets to the north of west, meaning it's visible for a longer period of time in the sky.summer solstice(June 20 or 21) is the longest day of the year, marking the start of summer.winter solstice(December 21 or 22)is the shortest day of the year, marking the start of winter

The highest peak of India.

Kanchenjunga is the highest peak of India and 3rd highest mountain all over the world.It is located in Sikkim.It has an elevation of 28,169 feet (8,586 metres).The mountain is part of the Great Himalaya Range.The name Kanchenjunga is derived from four words of Tibetan origin.

Moons of Mars

Mars has 2 moons ,Phobos and Deimos.Like Earth's Moon, Phobos and Deimos are also always presenting the same face to their planet. Both are lumpy, heavily-cratered and covered in dust and loose rocks.Mars is the fourth planet from the sun and also the 2nd smallest after mercury.It is also called as red planet

Longest road in the world

Spanning around 30,000 km, the Pan-American Highway is the longest road in the world. Starting from Alaska, the road moves south, passing through Canada, the United States, Mexico, and Central America.It was constructed in year 1936.
