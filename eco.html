Closed economy

An economy where there is no economic activities with an outside economy is a closed economy.In such economy there are no imports or exports.The goal of such economy is to provide consumers everything that they need from within the economy's border.Brazil is world's most closed economy.Brazil imports the least amount of goods.

Mixed economy

A mixed economy is a system in which both public and private sector exist.India is a mixed economy.This type of economy is generally under government regulation.The term mixed economy was given by JM keynes.

National statistical office

CSO(Central statistical organisation) was set up in 1950 .It was constituted to publish national income data. on the other hand NSSO(National sample survey organisation) was set up for conducting large scale sample survey to meet data needs of country.In may,2019 both CSO and NSSO were merged to form NSO (National statistical office).

NITI AAYOG

National Institution for Transforming India Aayog came into existnce on 1st January,2015.It is policy-making think-tank of government of India that replaced the Planning commission.It provides strategic and technical advice to Central and State Governments.

Agrarian economy

It is a type of economy that relies primarily on agriculture industry including livestock farming or crop production.It is a type of economy whose major factor of production is agricultural land.
